# epistemology-of-logic
Xenopoulos Mathematical Formalization of Logic-Dialectic and AI Applications
From Ancient Greek Philosophical Foundations to Modern Computational Intelligence

Abstract
This repository presents a complete computational formalization of Professor Epaminondas Xenopoulos' groundbreaking work "Epistemology of Logic: Logic-Dialectic or Theory of Knowledge" ((Kefalonia, GR, 1998; 2nd ed., 2024).We bridge 2500 years of Greek philosophical thought—from Parmenides and Heraclitus to Hegel and Marx—into modern mathematical frameworks, creating a novel approach to AI reasoning that handles contradiction, temporal dynamics, and qualitative transformation.

Why This Matters Now
The Crisis in Contemporary AI
Modern AI systems excel at pattern recognition but fundamentally fail at:
•	Handling contradiction (they crash or produce nonsense)
•	Understanding qualitative transformation (seed → plant, caterpillar → butterfly)
•	Reasoning through time-dependent truth values
•	Grasping dialectical processes inherent in biological, social, and cognitive systems
Xenopoulos' framework provides the mathematical backbone to overcome these limitations.
